About Dream Soccer Club

Dream Soccer Club is a grassroots football team supported by JOMPO Digital Solutions to nurture local talent, promote youth development, and bring communities together through sports. We believe sports can transform lives and build stronger societies.

With JOMPO's support, the club provides training, equipment, and mentorship to young players, helping them grow both on and off the field.
